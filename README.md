# ExpenseTracker

A simple and efficient Android application to track your daily expenses, manage budgets, and visualize your spending habits.

## 🚀 Features

- **Transaction Management**: Add, update, and delete your daily expenses with ease.
- **Categorization**: Organize your spending into categories like Food, Transport, Shopping, and more.
- **Monthly Overview**: View your total expenses and daily breakdowns for the current month.
- **Visual Analytics**: 
  - **Pie Chart**: See a percentage breakdown of expenses by category.
  - **Bar Chart**: Track daily spending trends throughout the month.
- **Budgeting**: Set and monitor your monthly budget to keep your finances on track.
- **Local Storage**: All data is stored securely on your device using Room Database.

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **Language**: Kotlin
- **Architecture**: MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel)
- **Database**: Room
- **UI Components**: Material Design, Jetpack Navigation, ViewBinding
- **Graphs**: [MPAndroidChart](https://github.com/PhilJay/MPAndroidChart) (if used) or custom implementation.
- **Dependency Injection**: Hilt/Koin (if applicable)
